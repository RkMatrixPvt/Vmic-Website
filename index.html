Academics

Vidya Mandir Inter College offers a well-rounded academic environment where students thrive across grades 6 to 12. Our intermediate programs span Humanities, Science, Commerce, Agriculture, and Vocational Studies‚Äîall delivered in Hindi by experienced faculty members dedicated to personalized learning.

The college features well-equipped science labs, a modern computer lab, a library with 300+ books, and a playground to support physical education. Students benefit from smart classroom initiatives and regular assessments designed to ensure exam readiness and academic excellence.

Admission

Eligibility:
  • Nursery: 3 years
  • LKG: 4 years
  • UKG: 5 years
  • Class 6: 11 years
  • Class 9: 14 years
  • Class 11: 16 years with minimum 75% in High School

Entrance Exam: Required for Classes 6, 9, and 11. Subjects include Hindi, English, Science, GK, and Math.

Documents Required:
  • Original Transfer Certificate
  • Marksheet (Especially for Class 11)
  • Age proof and other relevant documents

Selection Process: Based on entrance exam and interview (if required). Final decision rests with the Principal.

Admission Form: You can download the form from our website
